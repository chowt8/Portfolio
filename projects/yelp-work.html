Yelp • Product Designer • 2018—2019

People are searching for all kinds of things on Yelp, a restaurant that’s vegan, a reliable tailor, someone to fix that leaky faucet, the best undiscovered park in the city, a place that serves a good martini, really if you can think of it, you can Yelp it. I was lucky enough to work on the homescreen and search results experiences that takes in all these different types of search queries and routes you to the perfect business.
