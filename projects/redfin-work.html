Making home

renovations happen

Redfin • Senior Product Designer • 2020—Present

Sometimes a home needs a little TLC before it can be listed for sale, and that work can range from fresh paint, new flooring, swapping out old appliances, repairing the roof—the list goes on and on. With Redfin Home Services a seller can get a fully managed renovation plan, and listing prep with no money upfront until the home is sold. Since joining the team in 2020 I have worked on every piece of Home Services from the plan presented to the seller at the outset of a project, to the work list our field team and contractors rely on to make that plan reality. By building a deep understanding of this domain, I have been able to deliver design solutions that streamline essential workflows for a growing org of home renovation professionals.

As the Senior Product Designer on Home Services I have led a majority of the design execution, user research, and project prioritization since late 2020. A few key achievements from 2020—2022:

  • I overhauled our stage and status tracking system, eliminating our dependency on Zendesk for grainular status tracking.
  • I advocated for and designed a map view that gives our teams in the field a bird’s eye viewpoint of all the projects they are responsible for.
  • I revamped our photo taking and gallery experience on mobile and iPad making it faster and increasing its usability.
  • I helped reduce the time it takes to create and send project bids and contracts to a client by replacing a process that manually generates docs with one that is automated and editable.
