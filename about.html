ABOUT ME

I'm a designer of user interfaces, design systems, soft goods, & products for home. To get in touch, send an email to tatechow@gmail.com

Thanks for taking the time to read this short bio: I have professional experience as a designer working in product design for mobile and web, industrial design for home goods and handbags, illustrations for startups and brands and other exciting endeavors.

I was raised on the east coast in a suburb outside Philadelphia and graduated from Syracuse University in 2014 with a bachelors degree in Industrial & Interaction design.

Outside of product design I am designing my own brand of bags, and soft / hardgood accessories around the idea of helping people to go places and live comfortably.
