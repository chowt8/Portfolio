About

I have over 6 years of professional experience as a designer working in product design for mobile and web, industrial design for home goods and handbags, illustrations for startups and brands and other exciting endeavors. I was raised on the east coast in a suburb outside Philadelphia and graduated from Syracuse University in 2014 with a bachelors degree in Industrial & Interaction design. Outside of product design I am designing my own brand of bags, and soft / hardgood accessories around the idea of helping people to go places and live comfortably.
